## A Note From the Chapter

In the interest of transparency, this event was originally announced as a "raffle." We did not fully understand the legal meaning of that term and the regulations that accompany it, and have amended the name to "drawing" to accurately reflect our true intention. Updated rules for the drawing will be posted as soon as possible.

If you have already purchased a ticket and would like a refund, one will be remitted to you — simply reach out to us at [facebook.com/ecvlordsholtodouglasch3](https://www.facebook.com/ecvlordsholtodouglasch3), or in person at our Spring Doins. However, if you would prefer to let your contribution stand as a donation, it will be gratefully welcomed and put toward the drawing once we have the official rules ironed out.

We appreciate your patience and understanding, Brethren!
