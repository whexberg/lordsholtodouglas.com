Hear ye, hear ye! On the **second Tuesday of each month**, the esteemed officers of **Lord Sholto Douglas Chapter #3** convene in solemn council to deliberate upon matters of great import!

'Tis within these hallowed gatherings that the machinery of our Chapter turns — where budgets are balanced (or at least argued about), events are planned, and the noble work of E Clampus Vitus is set in motion!

---

### Matters Before the Council:

- **Reading of the Minutes** — A solemn recounting of the glorious absurdity that transpired at our last gathering
- **The Gold Dust Report** — An accounting of the Chapter's fortunes and fiscal standing
- **Event Planning & Logistics** — The who, what, where, and "wait, who's bringing the trailer?"
- **Charitable Works Oversight** — Widders, orphans, and historical markers await our attention
- **Membership & Administrative Matters** — The paperwork that keeps us legitimate
- **Long-Term Chapter Planning** — Scheming for the future glory of Clamperdom

---

While attendance is typically reserved for Board members and invited guests, know that every decision made within serves the greater good of the Chapter, our charitable endeavors, and the time-honored traditions we uphold.

Through careful planning and steadfast stewardship, the Board ensures that every event, donation, and gathering reflects the values and history we proudly carry forward!

**Credo Quia Absurdum!**
