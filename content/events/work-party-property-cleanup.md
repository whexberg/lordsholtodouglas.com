Hear ye, hear ye! **Lord Sholto Douglas Chapter #3** summons all able-bodied Brothers to muster at **Everybody's Inn** on **Saturday, April 4th** for our **Spring Work Party**!

First, we fill our bellies. Then, we ready the land for **Spring Doins**!

---

### The Day's Events

**Breakfast & Lunch Fundraiser** — At the crack of **7:00 AM** (ish), the Chapter shall be slingin' flapjacks and vittles to the good people of Foresthill. All proceeds benefit the Everybody's Inn (a long time sponsor of Lord Sholto Douglas #3). Bring thy appetite!

**Property Cleanup** — Once the breakfast crowd has been fed and watered, we ride out to prepare the Doins grounds. Brush to clear, camps to ready, and honest labor to be done!

### The Particulars

- **When:** Saturday, April 4th, 2026 — Muster at **7:00 AM**

### How to Help

All Brothers are encouraged to participate. Many hands make light work — and idle hands are the Devil's workshop! So, bring thy tools, thy gloves, and thy willingness to sweat!
