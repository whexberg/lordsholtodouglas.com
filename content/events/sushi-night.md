Because even the most grizzled of Redshirts deserves a night of culinary adventure! And more importantly, all proceeds from this evening of wasabi-induced tears and sake-fueled camaraderie shall flow directly into the coffers of **Lord Sholto Douglas Chapter #3**, supporting our charitable works, historical endeavors, and the general magnificent absurdity we so proudly uphold!

So sharpen thy chopstick skills, prepare thy palate for delights both familiar and exotic, and join thy Brethren for an evening of fine food and finer company!
