Hear ye, hear ye! Prepare thyselves for the legendary **Burning Mule** — an **8-Way Doins** bringing together **eight chapters** of E Clampus Vitus for a weekend of unprecedented brotherhood!

From **Friday, October 9th through Sunday, October 11th**, Redshirts from across the region shall converge for what promises to be one of the most magnificent gatherings of the Clampyear!

---

### What is an 8-Way Doins?

When eight chapters of E Clampus Vitus join forces, the result is a spectacle of camaraderie, tradition, and magnificent absurdity multiplied eightfold! Brothers from near and far shall gather to share stories, forge friendships, and celebrate the bonds that unite all who wear the Red Shirt!

---

### What to Expect

- **Eight times the brotherhood** — Meet Brothers from chapters across the region
- **Camping & Fellowship** — A weekend under the stars with kindred spirits
- **Traditional Clamper Activities** — Amplified by the presence of so many chapters
- **The Burning of the Mule** — A spectacle not to be missed!

---

### What to Bring

- Camping gear (tent, sleeping bag, chair)
- Warm layers (October nights are chilly!)
- Provisions and perhaps items to share
- Thy Chapter pride and spirit of adventure
