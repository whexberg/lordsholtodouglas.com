Hear ye, hear ye! By the hallowed traditions of the Ancient and Honorable Order, we proclaim the grand convocation of the **Annual Bean Feed and Hawkers Fair!** As is our most peculiar custom, we gather once more to partake in sustenance and the joyous clamor of the Hawkers Fair. Credo Quia Absurdum!

Join your fellow Brethren on **Saturday, February 7th, in the year of our Lord 2026 (or 6031, if you count in Clamp-lore)**. The festivities shall commence with the rising sun at **7:03 AM**, and shall not cease until the very last bean has fallen, or until our spirits are sated, whichever comes first!

**The Gathering Place:**

No mere hovel shall contain our revelry, but the venerable **Fairgrounds** itself! Seek out the grand locale at **1273 High Street, Auburn, CA 95603**.

### The Rub (Fees & Donations, if you must inquire)

- **Red Shirts (Honored Attendants):** A modest contribution of **$35 for the foresighted (prepay)**, or **$45 for those who arrive with impromptu zeal (at the door)**.
- **Cook Crews (Bearers of the Culinary Arts):** A pittance of **$33** per crew (comprised of three stout souls).

### For the Hawkers and Benefactors of the Raffle

Our tradition demands generous hearts! Contribute to the noble cause with an **Equal Value Raffle Donation:**

- **$35 Hawker**
- **$25 (1) Ass Hawker**

Come, partake in the merriment, the mystery, and the magnificent absurdity that only our esteemed order can provide. Let the beans be fed, the wares hawked, and the tales spun!

### Toy Drive for Koinonia Family Services

This year, we extend our benevolence beyond the brotherhood! We are collecting **new, unopened toys** (minimum $10 value) for **Koinonia Family Services**. Those who bring forth a charitable donation shall receive a **limited-edition commemorative patch** to mark their generosity in this special toy drive.

So bring your beans, your beards, your best hat, and your big heart—and join us for a day of good food, good deeds, and brotherhood at the Annual Bean Feed & Hawkers Fair!
