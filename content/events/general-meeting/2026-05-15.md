Hear ye, hear ye! On the **third Friday of each month**, all Redshirts in good standing are summoned to the **Grand Assembly** — where fellowship abounds, business is conducted, and the spirit of E Clampus Vitus burns ever brighter!

Whether thou art a grizzled veteran of countless Clampouts or a freshly-minted Brother still learning the ways, thy voice matters here! This is thy Chapter, and these gatherings keep us bound together in purpose and brotherhood.

---

### The Usual Order of Business:

- **Reading of the Minutes** — A solemn recounting of the glorious absurdity that transpired at our last gathering
- **The Gold Dust Report** — An accounting of the Chapter's fortunes and fiscal standing
- **Upcoming Events & Work Parties** — What's next, and who's bringing the shovels?
- **Charitable Works Updates** — Reports on our widders, orphans, and community efforts
- **Chapter Business & Voting Matters** — Democracy, Clamper-style (results may vary)
- **Historical Presentations & Local Lore** — Tales of the past, told with varying degrees of accuracy
- **Social Time & Fellowship** — The real reason we all showed up

---

Come forth, Brothers! Share thy voice, lend thy hand, and be part of the continuing saga of **Lord Sholto Douglas Chapter #3**. We gather to honor our history, serve our community, and keep the magnificent absurdity of E Clampus Vitus alive for generations to come!

**Credo Quia Absurdum!**
