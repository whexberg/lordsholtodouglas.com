Hear ye, hear ye! As the leaves begin to turn and the air grows crisp, the time approaches for **Fall Doins**!

From **Friday, September 18th through Sunday, September 20th**, Brothers shall gather for a weekend of camping, fellowship, and the time-honored traditions that make E Clampus Vitus the magnificent order it is!

---

### What to Expect

- **Camping under the autumn stars** — Bring thy tent, thy bedroll, and thy spirit of adventure
- **Brotherhood & Fellowship** — Stories told, friendships forged, and memories made
- **Traditional Clamper Activities** — The details of which shall remain between Brothers
- **Good Food & Better Company** — We feast as only Clampers can

---

### What to Bring

- Camping gear (tent, sleeping bag, chair)
- Warm layers (autumn nights get cold!)
- Provisions for thyself and perhaps to share
- A willingness to participate in the magnificent absurdity

---

This is a **members-only event**. If thou art not yet a Brother but wishes to join our ranks, speak with any Redshirt about becoming a Poor Blind Candidate!

**Credo Quia Absurdum!**
